SONGS

Genres We Play

Our four-piece band has great range of versatility with genres and instruments.
Listed below are some of our band configurations for each genre:

Classical: string quartet (two violins, viola, and cello)
Rock: guitar, bass, drums, keyboard (with tenor and soprano vocals)
Jazz: saxophone, piano, cello, flute, trumpet, drums
Country: guitar, fiddle, banjo


If you don't see your desired genre or a similar song listed below, we can also adapt as best as we can. Please understand that we are just four people so we only have four sets of hands that can play at the same time. We can pre-record different instruments ahead of time as backing tracks. Also, we can only sing in English (I mean, we can try to sing a different language if you REALLY want us to, but it's probably going to be mumbled all the way through).
